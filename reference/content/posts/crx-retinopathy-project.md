Have you or a family member been diagnosed with a CRX retinopathy?
Mutations in the gene CRX has been associated with dominant inherited retinopathies
- Retinitis Pigmentosa (RP) 
- Cone-Rod Dystrophy (CoRD) 
- Leber Congenital Amaurosis (LCA) 
Dr. Shiming Chen (Opthalmology & Visual Sciences – Washington University) has an exciting new study and is recruiting CRX families.
Check out [Dr. Chen Lab website](https://ophthalmology.wustl.edu/items/chen-lab/) to see the current projects.
If you are interested in participating in this study, please contact Dr. Shiming Chen ([chenshiming@wustl.edu](mailto:chenshiming@wustl.edu)) for further information
