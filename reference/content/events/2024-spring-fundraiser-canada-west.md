About the Event
This is our 4th Spring Fundraiser. Participants can walk, run, cycle, hike. Start when you want. Finish when you want. Distance doesn't matter - it's all for a good cause.
To participate:
1.) Register for the event.
2.) Join our [WhatsApp chat.](https://chat.whatsapp.com/BgeopyvnCWVEXMKk2gdn8o) [Click here.](https://chat.whatsapp.com/BgeopyvnCWVEXMKk2gdn8o)
3.) After registering, check your email Inbox from RP Hope with your race bib. Prior to race day, print your bib.
4.) On race day, bring the race bib as you walk, run, bike, swim, or hike. Take a selfie.
