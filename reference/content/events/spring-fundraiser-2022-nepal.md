About the Event
This is our 2nd Annual Spring Fundraiser. Participants can walk, run, cycle, hike. Start when you want. Finish when you want. Distance doesn't matter - it's all for a good cause. But, the person biking, walking, running or hiking to the creative destination "wins"!
To participate:
1.) Register for this event (no donation required).
2.) If you would like to join our virtual platforms, click on this link to our WhatsApp chat.
3.) After registering, check your Inbox for an email from RP Hope with your race bib.
4.) On the day of the event, bring the race bib, then take a selfie with this picture in the most creative spot on your walk, run, or ride.
