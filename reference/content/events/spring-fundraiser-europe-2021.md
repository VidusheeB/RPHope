About the Event
This is our first fundraiser for RP Hope. Participants can walk, run or cycle. Start when you want. Finish when you want. Distance doesn't matter - it's all for a good cause. But, the person biking, walking, or running to the creative destination "wins"!
What do you "win"? Our love and gratitude, of course, and an original painting by Cate. Details to follow.
